🐦 Birdwatching in Tanzania

Tanzania is home to over 1,100 bird species, making it a paradise for bird lovers. From the tranquil shores of Lake Duluti to the rich forests of Arusha National Park, every moment is a chance to witness unique and colorful species.

📌 Itinerary & Highlights
🌅

Early Morning: Begin your day with binoculars and a professional birding guide.

📍

Best Locations: Lake Duluti, Arusha National Park, or nearby reserves.

🦚

Highlights: Spot flamingos, turacos, hornbills, eagles, and other colorful species.

🧺

Afternoon: Enjoy a picnic by the lake or forest while continuing to watch rare and endemic birds.
