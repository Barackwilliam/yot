üß≥ Important Things to Pack for Climbing Mount Kilimanjaro

Preparing for your Kilimanjaro climb is more than just stuffing a bag‚Äîit‚Äôs about choosing the right gear that will keep you warm, dry, and energized from rainforest to the roof of Africa. Here's a complete guide to packing smart and safely.

üß• Technical Clothing

  • Insulated mid-layers: Fleece/down jackets for warmth.

  • Biodegradable wipes: For body cleaning in camps.
  • Hand sanitizer: Cleanliness when water is limited.
  • Lip balm with SPF: Prevent chapping and sunburn.
  • Toilet paper: Always carry your own!
üéí Essentials & Accessories
  • Backpack (30-40L): For day essentials.
  • Headlamp: Required for summit night.
  • Water bottles (3L total): Stay hydrated daily.
  • Energy snacks: Bars, nuts, chocolate.
  • Camera or GoPro: Capture your experience!
  • Power bank: Keep electronics charged.
  • Trekking poles: Support on steep descents.
  • Dry bags: Keep gear dry inside your pack.
