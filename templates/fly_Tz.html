How to fly to Tanzania Routes from all over the world

Sept 12, 2025

Flying to Tanzania is easier than ever, with convenient routes available from the U.S., Europe, and Asia to its main airports: Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), Abeid Amani Karume International Airport (ZNZ) in Zanzibar, and Julius Nyerere International Airport (DAR) in Dar es Salaam. Here’s an overview of popular and efficient routes from each region:

1. From the United States

There are no direct flights from the U.S. to Tanzania, but with a stopover, the journey is straightforward.

  • New York (JFK/EWR): Qatar Airways via Doha, Turkish Airlines via Istanbul, Emirates via Dubai.
  • Washington D.C. (IAD): Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa, Qatar Airways via Doha.
  • Los Angeles (LAX): Emirates via Dubai, Turkish Airlines via Istanbul.

2. From Europe

  • London (LHR/LGW): Qatar Airways via Doha, KLM direct, Turkish Airlines via Istanbul.
  • Amsterdam (AMS): KLM direct flights.
  • Frankfurt (FRA): Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa, Emirates via Dubai.
  • Paris (CDG): Air France/KLM via Amsterdam, Qatar Airways via Doha.

3. From Asia

  • Dubai (DXB): Emirates direct flights to DAR/JRO.
  • Doha (DOH): Qatar Airways flights with wide Asian connections.
  • Istanbul (IST): Turkish Airlines direct flights.
  • New Delhi (DEL): Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa, Qatar Airways via Doha.
  • Bangkok (BKK): Emirates via Dubai, Qatar Airways via Doha.

4. Domestic Connections within Tanzania

  • DAR → Zanzibar: Daily flights with Precision Air, Coastal Aviation, ZanAir.
  • DAR → Kilimanjaro: Daily flights via Precision Air and Air Tanzania.
  • Zanzibar → Kilimanjaro: Coastal Aviation connections available.

Tips for Booking

  • Book Early for Best Rates: Secure better rates by booking in advance.
  • Check Airline Alliances: KLM (SkyTeam), Qatar Airways (oneworld), and Turkish Airlines (Star Alliance).
  • Consider Multi-City Trips: Fly into Kilimanjaro and out of Zanzibar or Dar es Salaam to optimize travel.
